TeCanal Mount Hebron Scholarship

TeCanal is a charitable nonprofit organization that brings technology and STEM education to low-income communities in the Baltimore area. TeCanal was founded by four Mt. Hebron students six years ago, and most of its volunteers over the past six years have come from Mt. Hebron High School. This year, TeCanal is offering four $500 scholarships for students who are graduating from Mt. Hebron High School this year. The scholarship can be used to cover any educational expenses that the recipient may incur after graduating from Mt. Hebron High School. All 2021 graduates of Mt. Hebron High School are eligible for this scholarship, and previous involvement in TeCanal is not required.

TeCanal Executive Officer Scholarship

The TeCanal Executive Officer Scholarship is a $1,000 scholarship that can be used to cover any educational expenses that will be awarded to one person. All current and former executive officers of TeCanal who have not yet graduated from a four-year college/university are eligible for this scholarship.

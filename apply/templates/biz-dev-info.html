Business Development Internship

Five years ago, TeCanal was founded with the mission of using technology and STEM education to empower youth in low-income communities. Today, TeCanal serves over seventy children in some of the most impoverished communities in Baltimore.

This summer, TeCanal is running its first software engineering internship program. The internship will begin the week of July 6 and end the week of August 10 (6 weeks). The internship will be done remotely. Since we are a charitable non-profit organization, this is an unpaid volunteer internship.

Responsibilites:
  • Apply for grants
  • Plan potential fundraisers
  • Improve TeCanalâ€™s revenue model to sustain operations during COVID-19
  • Develop strategies TeCanal can use to better serve low-income neighborhoods during the COVID-19 pandemic
Requirements:
  • High school diploma
  • Demonstrated interest in business
Preferred Credentials:
  • Fundraising experience

To apply, please send a resume and cover letter to application@tecanal.org. The cover letter should explain why you want this internship and why you would be the ideal candidate for this position.

Applications are due June 30th, 2020. We review applications on a rolling basis before June 30, so we recommend submitting your application at your earliest convenience.

Please email us at application@tecanal.org if you have any questions.

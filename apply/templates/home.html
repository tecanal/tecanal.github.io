Join the TeCommunity

Apply to be a part of an organization run by students to benefit the community.


Four years ago, TeCanal was founded with the mission of using technology and STEM education to empower youth in low-income communities. Today, TeCanal serves over seventy children in some of the most impoverished communities in Maryland each year.

TeCanal is looking for new executive officers to lead TeCanal during the 2019-2020 academic year. You must be a rising sophomore, junior, or senior in high school to apply. All executive officers will be expected to volunteer at outreach sessions in Baltimore, MD regularly. Additionally, executive officers will have meetings at least twice a month. Some of these will be online, and some of these will be in-person in Howard County, MD.

We sincerely hope to appoint a new suite of executive officers who believe in the power of technology and STEM education in making our world a better place. Our vision is a world where everyone has the technology resources and STEM education they need to achieve their potential. We encourage you to be part of our next generation of leaders and help make TeCanal's vision come true!

Applications will close on Saturday, June 1st at 11:59 PM. Late applications will not be accepted.

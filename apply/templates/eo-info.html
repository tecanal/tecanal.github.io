Executive Officer

Applications are due by Monday, August 17th, 2020 at 11:59pm EST.

Email application@tecanal.org if you have any questions.

How to Submit An Application

  1. Answer the applications questions below in under 1,000 words combined and save it as a PDF.
  2. In the body of an email, write your name, school, and rising grade. Attach your application responses and send the email to application@tecanal.org.

Application Questions

Your answers to the four questions should be no greater than 1,000 words combined.

  1. What are you passionate about?
  2. What would you do with ten thousand dollars?
  3. What unique qualities and skills would you bring to TeCanal?
  4. How can TeCanal fulfill its mission during the COVID-19 pandemic?

What do Executive Officers do?

  • Communicate with community partners and forge new partnerships
  • Generate new ideas and initiatives to advance the organization
  • Write new curriculum for outreach sessions
  • Lead fundraising programs and apply for grants
  • Develop and maintain web content
  • Manage, represent, and recruit volunteers
  • Oversee and maintain TeCanal's presence on a variety of social media platforms
